About Me

Hi, I'm a PhD student at KU Leuven (at Embedded Systems and Security group, and in close collaboration with the research group COSIC) and work on FPGA design for network security, in collaboration with ETH Zurich on a joint research project. I previously worked as Research associate at Nanyang Technological University Singapore in the field of Deep learning and Embedded Systems, Business Intelligence Application Developer at IBM India, Embedded System programmer at Gemtech solutions Pvt Ltd (India), and as Lecturer at College of Engineering Kidangoor (India). I have completed my masters in Embedded System Design from NIT Kurukshetra , India, and my bachelors in Electronics and communication engineering from Cochin university of science and technology.

I'm very passionate about FPGAs, Convolutional Neural Networks, Network security, and the implementation aspects of CNNs and Network security algorithms on FPGAs.
